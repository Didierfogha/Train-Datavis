CO2 Emissions

An exploration of CO2 emissions trends and comparisons across various countries and regions.

CO2 Emissions per Capita

This section explores the amount of CO2 emissions produced on a per capita basis across different countries. Understanding the emissions per individual helps reveal the impact of lifestyles and consumption patterns on the environment.

Region vs Country

Here, we compare CO2 emissions from various regions and specific countries to highlight the differences in environmental impact between aggregated regions and individual nations.

Country Comparison

This section allows for a detailed comparison of CO2 emissions across multiple countries, focusing on metrics such as total emissions, emissions growth over time, and contributions to global emissions.
